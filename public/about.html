About Me

I’m a designer with a strong focus on web development. My background is in UX/UI, but I enjoy bringing ideas to life through code and learning how to make digital experiences both functional and beautiful.

Through this project, I wanted to push myself beyond design and dive into front-end development. It gave me the chance to understand how design decisions translate into structure, interactivity, and usability in a real product.

I’m still learning and experimenting, but I see coding as an extension of my design practice. My goal is to keep improving my development skills while continuing to create thoughtful, user-centered solutions.
